Benefits of Cloud Computing!!
Trade upfront/capital(CAPEX) expense for/with variable/operational(OPEX) expense
No need to heavily invest in data centers and servers
Can only pay when consuming computing resources
Pay only for how much you consume
Stop spending money to run and maintain data centers
Focus on projects that differentiate your business, not the infrastructure
Cloud computing lets you focus on your own customers, rather than on the heavy lifting of racking, stacking, and powering servers
Stop guessing capacity
Eliminate guessing on your infrastructure capacity needs
Prior decisions can lead to expensive idle resources or limited capacities
Can access as much or as little capacity as you need and scale up and down
Benefit from massive economies of scale
Achieve a lower variable cost than you can get on your own
Usage from hundreds of thousands of customers is aggregated in the cloud
Higher economies of scale with lower pay-as-you-go price
Increase speed and agility
IT resources are just a click away
Reduce the time to make those resources available to your developers
Dramatic increase in agility for the organization since the cost and time it takes to experiment and develop is significantly lower
Go global in minutes
Easily deploy your application in multiple regions around the world with just a few clicks
You can provide lower latency and a better experience for your customers at minimal cost
